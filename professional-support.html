Professional Support

Knowing When and How to Seek Help

When to Seek Support

While every child develops at their own pace, certain signs may suggest the need for evaluation or support:

  • Persistent difficulties in communication, motor, or social skills
  • Regression — loss of skills once acquired
  • Significant delays compared to age expectations
  • Emotional or behavioral concerns interfering with daily life

Local Professional Resources

  • Jefferson Healthcare Pediatric Clinic — Developmental screenings & referrals
  • Olympic Educational Service District 114 — Special education evaluations
  • Jefferson County Public Health — Early intervention programs
  • Private Providers — Speech, occupational, and mental health therapy available in Port Townsend and surrounding areas

Family Advocacy

Parents and caregivers are the most important advocates for their children. If concerns arise, trust your instincts and request formal evaluation through your school district or pediatrician. Remember: asking for help is a strength, not a weakness.
